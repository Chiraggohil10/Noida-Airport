Noida International Airport Achieves Another Milestone with Successful Calibration of Instrument Landing System

Noida/New Delhi, October 15, 2024: Noida International Airport (NIA) has successfully completed the calibration of the Instrument Landing System (ILS) and Precision Approach Path Indicator (PAPI). A Beech King Air 360ER from the Airports Authority of India (AAI) began the calibration process on Thursday, 10th October, with the process concluding on 14th October 2024.
